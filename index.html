Born August 17th 1989

Yung Court announces world tour! The top selling female artist of the year.Loved for her outstanding volcals, Yung Court is a multi-platinum arists with hits such as "Guac Life" and "Frose All Day".

As a young girl from Oakland Califronia, Yung Court was bound for stardom. She began her music career relatively young at age 15 she was singing in her friends apartment. On weekends she would perform at a local the "Karaoke shack". Blown away by her voice and thanks to exposure via social media, Courtney who would later take the stage name of "Yung Court" was offered studio time and began recording her music. With her dedication Yung Court landed a record deal with "Jam Sessions". Here she recorded her top songs "Guac Life" and "Frose All Day". In 2003 Yung Court moved to New York to help build her fan base and persue her dreams. With help from her record deal, Yung Court now has a upcoming world tour starting in New York and ending in her home town of Oakland Califronia.
